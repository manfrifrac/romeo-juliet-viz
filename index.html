Romeo and Juliet

Every time they meet

Whenever “Romeo” and “Juliet” appear in the play, a red line ties their names together. In a few seconds the whole tragedy turns into a dense, fragile web you can zoom into.

How it works Each “Romeo” is connected to every “Juliet”, drawn from the first scene to the last.
